About

I'm Daniel Siegle, a Bioinformatics Software Engineer based in North Carolina. I apply machine learning to drug discovery and development, with a focus on protein design, antibody engineering, and cheminformatics.

I founded Deep Learning RTP, a community meetup bridging academia and industry in the Research Triangle area. My work sits at the intersection of computational biology, deep learning, and pharmaceutical science.

I'm passionate about open science and sharing knowledge — from building neural networks from scratch to applying cutting-edge models to real-world drug discovery challenges.
